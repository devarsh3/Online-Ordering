Welcome to MD Burrito Help Page
1. Click on the Menu
2. Write your order in the "Order Here" section as it is on the menu and click Add
3. Your Order will appear on the right side of the Window with your Tax and Total Amount 
4. You can sort the menu by price by clicking on "Ascending" or "Descending"
5. After you're done ordering click on "Print Recipt" to confirm your order
6. Your order will Appear in a text file in the folder
